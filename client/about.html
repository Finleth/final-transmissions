Inspiration

Final Transmissions is a labor of love created by six developers in Irvine, California. We wanted to create an immersive storytelling experience, in which story settings are the real world, and your phone acts as a viewing portal into a different world. Ghosts seemed like the perfect conceit.

FAQ

  • How much does it cost?

    It's free!

  • Where can I get the card deck?

    You can download the card deck from the link above, in the cards section.

  • Where can I play?

    Check the stories page for a location near you.

  • How do I get more stories?

    More stories coming soon!

  • Why isn't my AR showing up?

    Your phone must be in landscape mode to see the augmented reality. Don't forget to bring your cards!

  • Does it work on iPhones?

    At the moment Final Transmissions is only available on Android devices.

  • What do ravens have to do with ghosts?

    Ravens are messengers to the afterlife.

  • How can I write my own story?

    Tools coming soon to write your own experience!
